The Resident

2018

"The Resident" is a medical drama television series that follows the story of Dr. Conrad Hawkins, a young and talented surgeon who begins his residency at a prestigious Atlanta hospital, where he uncovers the dark secrets and corruption that lurk beneath the surface of the hospital's glamourous façade.
