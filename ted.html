Ted

"Ted" is a TV series written and directed by Seth MacFarlane. The story follows a grown man who is best friends with his childhood teddy bear, which has come to life and is now a foul-mouthed and profane creature.

Join the hilarious adventures of Ted, a teddy bear who comes to life when no one is looking, and his best friend John, a human who has grown up with Ted.

2012
